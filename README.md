# echoburst 

the ultimate geometry dash brainrot engine. turns your skill issues into a cinematic masterpiece of loud noises and emotional damage

## what it does
* dynamic sounds based on your death percentage
* 0-10%: goofy sounds (bruh, oof, disconnect)
* 10-70%: mid-tier annoyance (fart, wth)
* 70-94%: pure sadness (violin)
* 95%+: metal pipe + xpshutdown + wikihow anger management redirect
